## Slide 2: Executive Summary
- AquaSense: An end-to-end framework for industrial and domestic water telemetry.
- Architectural Core: Heterogeneous sensor array (Flow, Ultrasonic) interfaced with ESP32 edge nodes.
- Intelligence Layer: Adaptive conservation algorithms based on real-time reservoir data.
- User Interface: Glassmorphic, real-time analytics dashboard with manual/automated override capabilities.
- Value Proposition: quantifiable reduction in water footprints and operational overheads.

## Slide 3: The Global Crisis & Local Inefficiency
- **The Challenge:** 40% of urban water supply is lost to non-revenue water (leaks/inefficiency).
- **The Gap:** Current systems are either purely manual or lack granular, zone-specific telemetry.
- **The Solution:** Granular monitoring at the faucet/zone level combined with automated valve actuation.

## Slide 4: Strategic Objectives
- **Telemetry Precision:** High-frequency sampling of flow rates and tank levels.
- **Autonomous Regulation:** Logic-driven valve positioning (SG90) to maintain hydraulic equilibrium.
- **Predictive Anomaly Detection:** Real-time leakage identification via consumption patterns.
- **Integrated Dashboarding:** Unified platform for multi-node management and historical reporting.

## Slide 5: Scope of Implementation
- **Current Deployment:** Scalable for residential complexes, educational institutions, and hospitals.
- **Technological Stack:** Edge Computing (C++/ESP32), Backend (Python/Flask), Frontend (ES6/Glassmorphism).
- **Data Persistence:** Lightweight CSV logging for high-performance retrieval.

## Slide 6: Literature Analysis & Benchmarking
- **Traditional Meters:** Static, manual, no alert system.
- **Existing IoT Meters:** Cloud-only, high latency, zero control actuation.
- **AquaSense Edge:** Low-latency local control loops with cloud synchronization.

## Slide 10: Hardware Specification (BOM)
- **Computing Unit:** ESP32 (Dual-core, Wi-Fi/BT).
- **Flow Ingestion:** YF-S201 Hall Effect Sensor.
- **Proximity/Level:** HC-SR04 Ultrasonic (1cm resolution).
- **Control Actuation:** SG90 Micro Servo for valve throttling.
- **Notification Interface:** 16x2 I2C LCD for local status.
